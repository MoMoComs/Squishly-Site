Design and build a visually stunning, modern landing page for a brand called “Squishly”.

Squishly is a Gen Z-focused brand that sells mystery boxes filled with squishy toys, stress balls, and sensory fidget items. The website should feel like a real startup brand, not a basic project.

-----------------------------------
DESIGN STYLE
-----------------------------------

The design should be:
- Playful but clean
- Soft and calming
- Trendy and Gen Z-focused
- Minimal but visually engaging

Use a pastel color palette:
- Primary: pastel purple (#c9a7ff)
- Secondary: soft pink (#ffd6f6)
- Accent: sky blue (#a7d8ff)

Design elements:
- Rounded corners everywhere
- Soft drop shadows
- Gradient backgrounds
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Floating shapes or blobs in the background

Typography:
- Bold, rounded headline font
- Clean, modern body font

-----------------------------------
HERO SECTION
-----------------------------------

Create a high-impact hero section:
- Large gradient background (purple to pink)
- Big title: “Squishly”
- Tagline: “Squeeze the Stress Away”
- Subtext: “Mystery boxes filled with satisfying squishy toys”
- Large CTA button: “Preorder Now”

Add:
- Floating shapes or animated blobs
- Optional mock product visuals
- Subtle motion (floating or bouncing elements)

-----------------------------------
PRODUCT SECTION
-----------------------------------

Design modern product cards for 3 boxes:

Mini Box
Medium Box (highlight as “Most Popular”)
Mega Box

Each card should include:
- Rounded card layout
- Soft shadow
- Clean spacing
- Product placeholder image
- Short description
- Price placeholder
- Button: “Preorder”

Add hover effects:
- Slight lift on hover
- Button animation

-----------------------------------
USER EXPERIENCE
-----------------------------------

- Smooth scrolling between sections
- Sticky navigation bar
- Clean spacing and alignment
- Clear visual hierarchy

Navigation:
- Home
- Shop
- How It Works
- Contact
- Preorder button on right

-----------------------------------
SECTIONS TO INCLUDE
-----------------------------------

1. Hero section
2. Product section
3. How It Works (3 steps with icons)
4. Benefits section (icons + short text)
5. Social proof section (reviews or TikTok-style layout)
6. Preorder form section
7. Call-to-action section
8. Footer

-----------------------------------
PREORDER SYSTEM
-----------------------------------

Instead of checkout:
- Create a clean, styled form

Fields:
- Name
- Email
- Box selection
- Quantity

Button:
“Reserve My Spot”

After submit:
Show confirmation message:
“You’re on the list.”

-----------------------------------
ANIMATIONS
-----------------------------------

- Fade-in sections on scroll
- Hover effects on buttons and cards
- Floating background elements
- Smooth transitions

-----------------------------------
MOBILE DESIGN
-----------------------------------

- Fully responsive
- Stack sections cleanly
- Large tap-friendly buttons
- Maintain spacing and readability

-----------------------------------
GOAL
-----------------------------------

The final website should:
- Look like a real modern startup
- Feel premium and visually appealing
- Appeal to teens and Gen Z
- Encourage users to preorder

Avoid:
- basic layouts
- harsh colors
- cluttered design
